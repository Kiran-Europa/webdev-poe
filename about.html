About Brew Haven

Founded in 2015 in Cape Town, Brew Haven Coffee Roasters is a family-owned coffee company passionate about sourcing, roasting, and serving only the finest beans. Our mission is simple: to deliver memorable coffee experiences while supporting sustainable farming communities.
